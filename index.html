Executive Summary

This analysis explores the pricing of AI-powered hotel reservation systems. The market operates primarily on a custom-quote basis, but clear patterns emerge. The most common approach is a tiered monthly subscription, with costs varying based on hotel size, feature set, and integration needs. This dashboard allows you to explore these factors to better understand the potential investment required.

Return on Investment (ROI)

Vendors justify their pricing by highlighting significant value propositions that can lead to a strong ROI.

Increased Direct Bookings: By capturing guests on your website, you bypass costly OTA commissions.

Operational Efficiency: Automating up to 80% of common guest queries frees up front-desk and reservation staff for higher-value tasks.

Enhanced Guest Experience: Providing instant, 24/7 answers to questions improves satisfaction and conversion rates.

Revenue Uplift: AI-powered tools can intelligently offer room upgrades, late check-outs, and amenities, increasing total revenue per guest.
